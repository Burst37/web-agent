# SAVO Example Runs

## Example 1 — Space Age AI Solutions

```yaml
project_read:
  business: Space Age AI Solutions
  industry: AI automation agency
  audience: local business owners and service businesses
  offer: landing pages, AI bots, automations, lead generation, appointment systems
  conversion_goal: book discovery call
brand_dna:
  clarity_score: 7
  authority_score: 7
  innovation_score: 10
  luxury_score: 8
  trust_requirement: high
  emotion_score: 7
  visual_risk_tolerance: high
  motion_tolerance: cinematic
creative_direction:
  route: VL-01 Dark Glassmorphism + cinematic SaaS proof
  attention: fullscreen video-first hero showing AI systems operating for real businesses
  story: problem → automation system → booked appointments → client growth
  layout: dashboard expansion + pinned process + proof bento
  motion: GSAP ScrollTrigger for process, Motion for cards/buttons, reduced-motion fallback
  material: off-black #050508, glass panels, ice-blue/chrome-cyan accents (#8CB8CC), data labels
trust_strategy:
  required: case studies, screenshots, process transparency, client-use examples
conversion_strategy:
  primary_CTA: Book AI Growth Audit
  secondary_CTA: See Automation Systems
  lead_capture: calendar + Vapi voice agent + GoHighLevel follow-up
rejected:
  - generic SaaS blobs
  - fake metrics
  - centered hero with three cards
```

## Example 2 — Encore Home Care Services

```yaml
project_read:
  industry: home healthcare
  audience: families seeking in-home care
  conversion_goal: schedule consultation or call
brand_dna:
  trust_requirement: critical
  innovation_score: medium
  emotional_score: high
  visual_risk_tolerance: low_to_medium
creative_direction:
  route: warm trust + restrained premium healthcare
  attention: calm full-screen hero video with caregiver/client warmth
  story: family concern → compassionate support → safe care plan
  layout: trust-first hero, service clarity cards, caregiver process, testimonials, final CTA
  motion: soft fades, gentle parallax only, no aggressive scroll scrubbing
trust_strategy:
  proof_before_offer: true
  assets: testimonials, service area, care values, certifications, contact visibility
conversion_strategy:
  primary_CTA: Schedule Care Consultation
  secondary_CTA: Call Now
  mobile_CTA: sticky call button
rejected:
  - heavy liquid glass
  - nightclub-style motion
  - futuristic dashboard UI
```
